About

The Pittsburgh Purchasing Suite is a collection of apps to help purchasers. It includes Scout, a tool for discovering items that are on contract right now, and Beacon, a tool for City Purchasers to advertise new opportunities and businesses to sign up to receive information about those opportunities.
